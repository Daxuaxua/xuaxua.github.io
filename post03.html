Graphic Design

by xuxue / 2 weeks ago

一个简单的药品使用场景. 通过手机软件提醒用户吃药时间和药品信息的3D插画. 简单明快的3D画面和文字更加直观的传达信息.

使用Cinema 4D完成建模和场景搭建，使用Anorld完成渲染，后期通过Photoshop完成图片的调色和调整.


A simple drug use scenario. 3D illustration reminding users of the time to take medicine and drug information through mobile phone software. Simple and lively 3D pictures and text convey information more intuitively.

Use Cinema 4D to complete the modeling and scene building, Anorld to complete the rendering, and Photoshop to complete the color blending and adjustment of pictures.
