Bedroom


The Process

一个正在休息的场景. 疫情期间，我制作了这个卧室场景插画. 丰富的色彩搭配、织物绵软的材质营造在家里轻松舒适的环境,鼓励大家疫情期间待在家里，保持好社会距离.

I made an illustration of this bedroom scene during the epidemic, in which rich color matching and soft fabric materials create a relaxed and comfortable environment at home, encouraging everyone to stay at home during the epidemic and maintain a good social distance.
