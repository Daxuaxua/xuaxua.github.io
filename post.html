Character Design

by xuxue / 1 month ago

一个有趣的头脑风暴的场景和3D动画角色插画. 明亮的色彩搭配和卡通的人物角色带来很好的视觉吸引和活力. 试图营造一种和谐的讨论场景. 提醒人们保持积极的讨论.

使用Cinema 4D完成角色的建模和场景搭建，使用Anorld完成渲染，后期通过Photoshop完成图片的调色和调整.


A fun brainstorming scene and illustration of 3D animated characters. Bright color pairings and cartoon characters bring great visual appeal and energy. Try to create a harmonious discussion scene. Remind people to keep the discussion positive.

Use Cinema 4D to complete the role modeling and scene building, use Anorld to complete the rendering, and use Photoshop to complete the color blending and adjustment.
